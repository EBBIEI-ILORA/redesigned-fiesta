Questions we hear often

If yours isn't here, reach out—we love hearing from you.

Does WanderlustFin move or transfer my money?

No—and that's intentional. WanderlustFin is a visibility and strategy tool, not a transactional one. We illuminate your finances so you can make empowered decisions. Your money stays exactly where it is; we just help you see it clearly.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption and never store your login credentials. We partner with Plaid for secure account connections—the same technology used by major financial institutions.

What's the wealth archetype quiz about?

Think of it like an Enneagram for your money relationship. The quiz reveals your dominant wealth pattern—Builder, Visionary, Nurturer, or Maverick—and your AI advisor uses this to personalize every insight to how you actually think and operate.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier?

Yes, anytime. Your data and progress stay with you. If you upgrade, you get immediate access to new features. If you downgrade, you keep access until your current billing period ends.

I'm not a business owner. Is this still for me?

Yes. While we built WanderlustFin with entrepreneurs in mind, anyone building toward financial freedom will benefit. If you have goals, income, and a desire to understand your money better, you're exactly who this is for.
