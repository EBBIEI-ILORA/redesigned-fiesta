Questions we hear often

If yours isn't here, reach out—we love hearing from you.

Does WanderlustFin move or transfer my money?

No—and that's intentional. WanderlustFin is a visibility and strategy tool, not a transactional one. We illuminate your finances so you can make empowered decisions. Your money stays exactly where it is; we just help you see it clearly.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption and never store your login credentials. We partner with Plaid for secure account connections—the same technology used by major financial institutions.

What's the wealth archetype quiz about?

Think of it like an Enneagram for your money relationship. The quiz reveals your dominant wealth pattern—Builder, Visionary, Nurturer, or Maverick—and your AI advisor uses this to personalize every insight to how you actually think and operate.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier?

Yes, anytime. Your data and progress stay with you. If you upgrade, you get immediate access to new features. If you downgrade, you keep access until your current billing period ends.

I'm not a business owner. Is this still for me?

Yes. While we built WanderlustFin with entrepreneurs in mind, anyone building toward financial freedom will benefit. If you have goals, income, and a desire to understand your money better, you're exactly who this is for.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is WanderlustFin?

WanderlustFin is a wealth identity platform that helps entrepreneurs and high-earners see their complete financial picture, understand their money patterns, and build wealth with clarity and intention.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use Plaid for all bank connections — the same technology trusted by Venmo, Robinhood, and major financial institutions. We never store your bank login credentials.

What's a Wealth Archetype?

Your Wealth Archetype is your unique relationship pattern with money. Understanding it helps you make aligned decisions and build habits that match who you're becoming as a wealth steward.

When is WanderlustFin launching?

We're currently in pre-launch. Join our waitlist to get early access and lock in founding member pricing before we open to the public.

What accounts can I connect?

Via Plaid, you can connect checking accounts, savings accounts, high-yield savings (HYSA), investment/brokerage accounts, credit cards, and many insurance accounts.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes! We offer a 14-Day Clarity Guarantee. If WanderlustFin doesn't bring you more financial clarity within 14 days, we'll refund your first month.

Is this a budgeting app?

Not exactly. Traditional budgeting apps focus on restriction. WanderlustFin focuses on expansion, identity, and becoming the person who builds wealth — not just someone who tracks spending.

Can I use this for my business finances?

Yes! Our Business Goal Tracker (available in Expansion tier and above) lets you track revenue goals, program launches, and business milestones alongside personal finances.
